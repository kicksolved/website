The Solution: New Business in 1, 2, 3

  1. Employees & customers brainstorm problems for a specific device category. Customers validate if a problem is fake [Kick] or real [Solve].
  2. Solutions are proposed and either Kicked or Pre-Ordered.
  3. If enough pre-order, the solution is made. Solutions are delivered via app stores on smart devices. App subscriptions can subsidize the device and allow for higher hardware margins and customer lock-in.
